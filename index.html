About Me

Eri-ifeoluwa Odeyemi

I’m a student of  Industrial design based in Savannah GA. My work is focused on producing quality results and making a significant impact. I’m always happy to apply the experience and skills I’ve obtained throughout my life and college career to an interesting project, especially one that involves creative problem solving and innovative thinking.

My passion is to improve the lively hood of all men through the power of industrial design, breaking through the barriers created by habituation, and improving the relationships between people and the products they use. As a prospective industrial designer I aim to make People stop and look at my artistic and "out of the box" approach to design, however, I also aim to focus on the user experience, quality, and functionality of every product I design.

I strongly believe in the power of design and its ability to turn a simple product into a meaningful experience. Have a look at all I’ve worked on, and let me know if you have any questions.
